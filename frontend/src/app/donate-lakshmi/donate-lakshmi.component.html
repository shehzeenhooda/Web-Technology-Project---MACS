Why to Donate?

Your donation will help a pet. We provide emergency veterinary care and pet food to the hundreds of thousands of pets that are homeless. Many pets are given up because their owners are no longer able to care for them, financially or behaviorally. We rescue pets that are surrendered to us, that shelters turn away, that are found in empty apartments and foreclosed homes and even dumpsters, or that were released into the wild completely unable to survive on their own.
